ABOUT SUNFINDER

Sunlight, block by block.

Sunfinder’s Python service calculates the sun direction for Helsinki, fetches building footprints, and projects each estimated building shadow. Your browser renders the interactive map.

In live mode, current cloud cover softens or hides the projected shadows. At a selected time, shadows are clearly marked as clear-sky potential. Building footprints load from OpenStreetMap when available. Shadow shapes are an estimate, designed for finding pleasant sun rather than surveying or safety decisions.
